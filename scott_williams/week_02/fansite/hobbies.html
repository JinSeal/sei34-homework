Hobbies of Sophie Wilson

Sophie Wilson loves her digital camera (she could be biased because the camera is probably driven by the circuit boards she first designed back in 1985).

She is involved in your local theatre group and has acted in a number of productions.

Sophie has been known to look after a children's creche at the Hartington Grove Quaker Sunday morning meeting.

She likes gardening. To be truthful, she likes others getting in and helping her garden. One of her favourite garden modifications is the pond and bridge addition to her backyard.
